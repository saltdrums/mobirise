Drum Hoops

100% Birch & Maple

We offer a wide variety of drum hoops for bass drums, tom toms, and snare drums in both Birch & Maple. Bass drum counter hoops are cut from Birch or Maple Keller hoop stock cylinders in 10ply or 12ply. All tom and snare wood hoops and rims are made in house using Keller Maple stock.

Hide-A-Head Hoops

Classic

Avaiable in a range of finish options these classic style hoops offer a modern look by hiding the collor of the drum head, hence the name Hide-A-Head.  

Practical

Unlike most we strictly use USA stock to craft our hoops as thse create a durable long lasting hoop. While these are stronger than import hoops seen on budget drums they are made of wood & will break when aboused just like any other instrument.  While Hide-A-Head hoops on toms & kick will hold up to heavy hitters, the snare are best suited for dynamic arsts.    

Sounds

Wood hoops not only look outstanding but greatly add to the timber of the instrument.
