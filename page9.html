The Bearing Edge Service

Simular the bridge of a guitar, the bearing edge is the meaditor who gets all the action. By sculpting the profile of the edge we can balance drum shell tone & drum head tone. SALT is proud to offer all edges styles from blunt to acoute. 

1.

Square Edge

First each side of the drum shell is sanded true & square to ensure a flat surface. 

2.

Percision Edging

Second the edge is percision cut to the desired profile.  SALT is proud to offer all edges styles from blunt to acoute. 

3.

3 Step Sanding

Third the edge is hand sanded with a three step pregression for a smooth surface. 

4.

SALT  Edge Polish 

Lastley, each edge is treated with our secret polish to reduce friction & ease the tuning process.

*ACRYLIC shells undergo an aditional secret polish to return the "glass like" clarity.
