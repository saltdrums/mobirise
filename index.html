 What is SALT?

Specializing in wood hoops, handcrafted snare shells & acoustically engineered kits, owner Shawn Ryan combines classic craftsmanship, innovative sound design, & personolized service to "produce" drums in Orange County California. 

Is SALT for me?

SALT is for those who desire to work directly with a creator to develop & express ones individualized sound & style.  SALT is an indipendent American Small Business & a brand who believes in equal opportunity. 

Why SALT?

They are just drums... 
"Regardless of lable & legality, the drummer is the authority who often sits on a throne to lead melody, leric, & audiance.  It is my pleasre to equip these leaders with the tools to do just that."

What sets you apart from other qualified brands?

"While I realize that there are others who import similar product, what sets me apart is  over a decade of real world experience.  I combine Sonic, Artistic, Leadership & Tatical skills to deliver results for those in both live & studio settings." 

So what's the end goal?
"At SALT we believe music is key to healthy IQ, EQ, & personal development at any age."

"You can't hide behind them, drums are not only heard but felt."

At SALT, All drums are built using top shelf American Made Shells; Utilizing his experienced ears and eyes Ryan hand selects the cream of the crop to produce each instrument. With a range of shell material, size, & thickness in grasp, we can be quite intentional & produce the right instruments for your specific situation.

All metal & acrylic snare drums offered by SALT are built in-house using premium U.S. raw materials.
